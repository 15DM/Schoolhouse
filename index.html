The Schoolhouse

Bar & Restaurant

Take a trip down memory lane...

The Schoolhouse was founded at the start of 2015 and is a restaurant/bar located in Battersea, London.

Whether it’s a distant memory or a recent fond farewell, prepare yourself for a fun filled trip back to the best bits of your school days! Providing the solution to the South London social equation, TSH sets the standard of excellence. Whether it be for breakfast or brunch, school lunch and dinners or maybe just a late night cocktail, we tick all the boxes and pass with flying colours.

With a confiscated collection of craft beers, a very naughty wine list, a top class meat smoker and even a science lab of weird and wonderful cocktails, TSH is the nostalgic saunter down memory lane that most definitely “exceeds expectations”.
